Some random information

Le Havre can be considered to be Paul Pogba's first professional club. Pogba captained their youth team and established himself as a well known talent

Pogba's time in Juventus was probably the highlight of his career, helping Juventus win two consectuve Serie A titles and cementing his role in the team.

Pogba came through Manchester United's youth system and after leaving Juventus he joined United's first team. He has been an influential player for them.

Pogba has also been very successful with his international team France. He has captained the youth teams and helped France with the World Cup in 2018.

“The real Pogba is the one you see every time. You know, when I’m on the pitch, I cannot act. I’m not an actor. So when I’m in the pitch, I like to joke and laugh, and outside the pitch, I’m the same. For me, I’m normal. I come and play football. I do what I love.”

- Paul Labille Pogba
